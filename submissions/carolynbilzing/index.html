What have I done to get me here?

Long...long time ago...I had a vision of how I thought my life would be...I had it all figured out...but life had another path...

I started out traveling...living how I wanted to live...no one could tell me what to do...I was my own person...not a care in the world...

Until I met the two loves of my life...my children. I thought I knew what I was doing...I had no idea...Gabrielle Margaret "Gabby" was born October 18, 1997 and Samuel Nicholas "Sam" was born July 13, 1997. I can't imagine what my life would be without them...
